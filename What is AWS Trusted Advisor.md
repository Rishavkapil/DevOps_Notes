
AWS Trusted Advisor is a tool that provides real time guidance to optimize your AWS infrastructure for better performance, security and cost savings. 

It continuously analyzes your environment against AWS best practices, identifies opportunities for improvement, and recommends specific actions to take. 

#### How it works 

* **Analyzes your environment :** Trusted advisor scans your AWS account to evaluate your resources and configurations 

* **Checks against best practices :** It checks your environment against best practices in several categories : cost optimization, performance , security etc. 

* **Provides recommendations :** Based on its analysis, it provides actionable recommendations, you can optimize your cloud infrastructure, close security gaps etc.
